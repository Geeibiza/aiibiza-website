Section 02 — The Regulatory Problem

The AEPD Has Already Ruled on ChatGPT

This is not a theoretical risk. The Agencia Española de Protección de Datos has published explicit guidance on the use of cloud AI tools with personal data. Using ChatGPT, Claude.ai, or any cloud-based AI with client financial data is a documented violation.

AEPD — Agencia Española de Protección de Datos

Explicit Guidance on Cloud AI with Personal Data

The AEPD has published guidance establishing that using cloud AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude.ai, Microsoft Copilot, etc.) with personal data — including client NIFs, income data, bank account details, tax returns, and employment records — constitutes a breach of GDPR Article 28 (processor obligations) if proper Data Processing Agreements are not in place, and potentially Article 44 (international transfers) for US-based providers.

The AEPD began formal investigations into AI provider data flows in 2023. Published guidance updated 2025. Fine enforcement began Q1 2025.

Section 04 — What Private AI Does For Gestorías

From Document Avalanche to Automated Despacho

Private AI does not replace the gestor. It eliminates the hours-per-day that gestores spend on mechanical tasks — document extraction, drafting standard responses, monitoring regulations — so they can focus on advisory work that actually requires expertise.

01

Document Intelligence: Invoice OCR & Auto-Entry

Upload a batch of facturas — scanned PDFs, photos from WhatsApp, digital invoices. The AI extracts proveedor, NIF, importe, IVA, fecha, and concept. It validates against your client list and flags anomalies. It exports directly in A3/Sage import format. What takes a staff member 4 hours now takes minutes.

Before: Manual key-in, 4+ hours/batch
After: Upload & review, 15–20 minutes
02

Alegaciones Drafting: 4–8 Hours to 45 Minutes

When Hacienda sends a requerimiento or propuesta de liquidación, the gestor feeds the document to the AI along with the client file. The AI drafts a complete alegación — citing jurisprudencia, referencing applicable artículos of the LGT, LIRPF, or LIVA, and structuring the argumentación correctly. The gestor reviews and signs. A task that consumed a full working day now takes under an hour.

Before: 4–8 hours per alegación
After: 45 minutes including review
03

BOE Monitoring: Automatic Daily Regulatory Watch

The system monitors the Boletín Oficial del Estado daily. It extracts relevant changes to IRPF, IVA, IS, cotizaciones, and professional regulations. It flags items that affect your client portfolio specifically — not generic tax alerts, but targeted analysis of what matters to your despacho's client mix. No more manual BOE reading. Regulatory changes surface automatically.

Before: 30+ min/day, or missed entirely
After: Automated summary in morning briefing
04

Client Communications: Auto-Draft Standard Responses

The AI reads incoming client emails and WhatsApp forwarded messages, identifies what is being asked (missing factura, explanation of a liquidación, status update), and drafts a professional response in correct Spanish. The gestor approves and sends. 2 hours of email per day becomes 15 minutes of reviewing drafts.

Before: 2–3 hours/day reading and writing
After: 15–20 minutes reviewing AI drafts
05

Deductibility Analysis: Maximise Client Outcomes

Feed the client's expense list to the AI. It categorises each item against applicable IRPF/IS deductibility rules, flags expenses that require documentation, identifies opportunities the client has not captured, and produces a structured summary. Better outcomes for clients, achieved faster, with less senior time.

Before: Senior gestor reviews manually
After: AI pre-analyses, gestor validates
